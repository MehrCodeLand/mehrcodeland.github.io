About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer with a passion for solving complex problems through artificial intelligence. With a background in Computer Engineering, I specialize in Natural Language Processing, Image Processing, and Convolutional Neural Networks.

My journey in AI began during my studies, where I developed a fascination with how machines can be trained to understand and interpret the world around us. Since then, I've worked on various projects that leverage cutting-edge techniques to create practical solutions.

I'm fluent in German and English, which allows me to collaborate with international teams and access a broader range of research and resources in the field.

When I'm not coding or training models, you'll find me exploring my other passion: photography. I believe that photography and machine learning share many principles - both involve capturing patterns, understanding composition, and interpreting visual data.
